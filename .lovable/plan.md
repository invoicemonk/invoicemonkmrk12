

# Invoicemonk Hero Section Plan

## Overview
A sleek, interactive hero section that combines the friendly personality of your monk mascot with professional invoicing credibility. The design will use your red brand color as an accent against a clean, modern layout.

---

## Layout & Structure

### Navigation Bar
- Invoicemonk logo (the monk mascot + wordmark) on the left
- Clean navigation links: Features, Pricing, About
- "Get Started" CTA button on the right
- Subtle blur effect on scroll

### Hero Content (Two-Column Layout)
**Left Column - Messaging:**
- Bold headline with animated text reveal: "Invoicing Made Simple"
- Subheadline explaining the value proposition
- Two CTA buttons: "Start Free" (primary red) + "Watch Demo" (outline)
- Trust badges/social proof: "Trusted by 10,000+ businesses"

**Right Column - Interactive Visual:**
- Animated invoice mockup with floating elements
- Subtle 3D tilt effect on hover
- Animated checkmarks, payment status, and numbers
- Floating decorative elements (coins, checkmarks, the monk waving)

---

## Interactive Elements

1. **Animated Text** - Headline words fade in sequentially
2. **Floating Graphics** - Invoice card with gentle bobbing animation
3. **Hover Effects** - Buttons with smooth scale and glow effects
4. **Parallax Elements** - Decorative shapes that move slightly on mouse movement
5. **Gradient Background** - Subtle animated gradient in the brand colors

---

## Design Style

- **Colors**: Red primary (#DC2626) with white background and soft gray accents
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans-serif with bold headlines
- **Feel**: Playful yet professional - matching your friendly monk mascot
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first with stacked layout on smaller screens

---

## Deliverables

1. Hero component with all animations
2. Updated color theme to match Invoicemonk branding
3. Logo integrated into the header
4. Fully responsive design
5. Smooth micro-interactions throughout
